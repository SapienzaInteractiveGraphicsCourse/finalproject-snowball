Snowball



Welcome in Snowball!
In this game you impersonate a Snowman's head
that has to cross all the Ski run, trying to avoid
all the trees and rocks that will appear in the path,
in order to reach the finishing line.
During the run you have to pay attention also to the Ski run's limits.
You can also try to collect coins that will improve your final score.
Have fun!
